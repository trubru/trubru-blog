Front End NE Talk

Back in August 2019, I did my first ever talk at FrontEnd NE. I talked about the process I went through when designing and developing the website. This included the personas I created for the stakeholders to determine what content should go on the website and then on to the technical requirements - such as considering if a cms was required.

This talk was to by far the biggest audience I had ever spoken to, and was scary at first. I don't remember much about the talk itself, but I think it was well received based on the kind comments I received afterwards.

Thanks to FrontEnd NE for having me.
